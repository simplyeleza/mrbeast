About Mr Beast

Jimmy Donaldson, popularly known by his netizen name MrBeast is a 23-year-old YouTube content creator and internet sensationist. With a whopping 84 million subscribers on his main YouTube channel, Jimmy can comfortably lay claim to be the 'Godfather of content creation.’ MrBeast kick-started his career in 2012, at the early age of 13. In 2019 Donaldson posted on his Twitter handle: ‘I started at 13 years old with no money, a horrible laptop, and no microphone. Everyday for 8 years, I have obsessed and reinvested everything I have made. I am grateful to wake up happy and excited everyday and all I want in life is to make the best content possible MrBeast would then have to wait until 2017 to get his major breakthrough. It was after his ‘counting to 100,000’ video went viral and garnered thousands of views just days after posting.
